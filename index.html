About me

My name is Roselynn Frempong, an early childhoood educator who is currently workiing a charter school with over 3 years experience in the education field. With my background in political science and education , I incorporate civic engagement and my expertise in both general and special education through project based learning to go beyond traditional teacher practices. As an culturally responsive educator, I regularly frequent social and cultural trainings to help deepen my understanding of the communities I teach in. My goal is to provide children with the necessary support to improve on skills. Kindly contact me for a tutor if you have anyone in need of this service for their child.
